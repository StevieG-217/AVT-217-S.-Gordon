Good Portfolio Examples

Sam Kaufmann

Portfolio

While their homepage seemed a little cluttered at first glance the colors of the art complimented the white background very nicely. Clicking on a piece, will take you to (pictured) a simple 2 column layout featuring the artwork and a short description. I like how this artist uses negative space to keep their website very crisp and clean looking.

Beeper

Portfolio

This artist from the Netherlands, who goes by the pen name: Beeper, has a minimalist One Column Website. Examples of art are presented at first glance and simplistic icons serve as links to the Artist’s social media where more art is displayed. Overall, I think the artist made good use of the negative space on the home page, especially with the gradient background. It compliments the cover work nicely.

Kerby Rosanes

Portfolio

Rosanes has worked for so many Big Name Companies so his portfolio website, which uses a third column grid, is obviously a professional showcase. However, when exploring the website it proves itself to be much more extensive/interactive. For Example, when you click on an art piece it will lead you to a separate page for more details regarding that art, and if you can purchase it as a print or even a temporary tattoo. Furthermore, since this artist mostly works in black and white ink, his red logo really draws attention on the home page.

Rahul Noronha

Portfolio

Like Rosanes, Noronha uses a grid to display their art on the homepage, however, unlike Rosanes the grid is asymmetrical and the title isn't listed beneath the work, you have to click on it to access it.  Still, I think the page has a very neat and modern design. The negative space in between the photos creates a nice border for them and the variety of mediums of art showcases the artist’s ability.  

Anwita and Arun

Portfolio

This Creative Duo, based in India, uses a very similar grid system for their work presented on their homepage as the other artists I’ve reviewed. Again, the negative white space makes for a nice border and contrasts the pictures nicely. The site has a very clean modern style using a two column web grid.
